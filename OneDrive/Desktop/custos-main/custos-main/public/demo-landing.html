Capabilities
Built to catch what
matters most.
AI
Real-time YOLO Detection
YOLOv8n runs in your browser via WebAssembly. Camera feed never leaves your device — zero uploads, zero privacy risk.
ZN
Custom Zone Drawing
Draw HIGH SECURITY or OBSERVATION zones by dragging on the live feed. Alerts only fire inside zones you define.
RS
Risk Score Engine
A live 0-100 score tracks pacing, crouching, lingering, freezing, running, and repeated entries to detect anomalies.
AL
Instant Alerts
Alert banner fires with sound the moment risk crosses your threshold. No delay, no lag on the notification.
EV
Evidence Gallery
Every alert saves a clean screenshot and a 10-second video clip you can download instantly — no boxes on the photo.
TM
Tamper Detection
Detects camera cover or obstruction using brightness and image uniformity analysis. Fires a tamper alert immediately.
How it works
Up and running
in 4 steps.
01
Connect Camera
Plug in any USB webcam or use your built-in camera. Browser asks for permission once.
02
Draw Zones
Click and drag on the live feed to draw HIGH or OBSERVATION zones where you want monitoring.
03
Start Monitoring
Click Monitor. YOLO starts detecting people and tracking their behaviour in real time.
04
Get Alerts
Anomaly detected - alert fires with sound, clean screenshot, and 10-second video clip.
